Our Academics

SSC Board Affiliated • Comprehensive Curriculum for Holistic Development

Pre-Primary

(Nursery, LKG, UKG)

Play-based learning with Montessori methods, focusing on social skills, language development, and early numeracy through joyful activities and creative exploration.

Primary School

(Classes 1–5)

Foundation building with integrated English, Mathematics, EVS, Arts, and Physical Education. Emphasis on conceptual understanding, critical thinking, and value education.

Upper Primary

(Classes 6–8)

Subject specialization begins with Science, Social Studies, Mathematics, Languages. Project-based learning, skill development, and preparation for board pattern.

Secondary School

(Classes 9–10)

SSC Board preparation with rigorous academics, competitive exam coaching, career guidance. Focus on board exam excellence while maintaining holistic development.
